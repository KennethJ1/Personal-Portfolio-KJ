CampusGigs

Created during the hackED 2025 hackathon hosted by the Computer Engineer Club @UofA Idea, was that it was to help connect students in order to help them with three main jobs types: 1. Academic Help - Connecting students with peers who have expertise in a subject, providing an alternative to TA-led support that sometimes falls short. 2. Food Delivery by Foot - A student-driven way to grab meals for others nearby, ensuring fair pay and reducing reliance on costly third-party services. 3. Creative Services - A space for students to hire photographers, designers, or other creatives, whether for grad photos or personal projects. To bring this idea to life, we built a full-stack web application using MongoDB Atlas for data storage, Flask with PyMongo for backend development, and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the user interface. By the end of the hackathon, we had a functional platform where users could register, log in, and create job posts, with room for future growth and refinement.
